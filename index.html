Our Services

24/7 emergency services available for CT Scan, X-Ray & MRI.

X-Ray

High-quality digital X-ray imaging for clear and precise diagnosis of bone and soft tissue conditions.

Ultrasound & 4D Doppler

Advanced ultrasound and 4D color doppler services for real-time imaging of organs, tissues, and blood flow.

CT Scan (4D Multi-Slice)

State-of-the-art 4D multi-slice CT scanner for detailed cross-sectional images of the body.

Echocardiography

Comprehensive cardiac ultrasound to assess the heart's structure and function.

FNAC & Biopsy

CT and USG guided procedures for accurate sample collection and diagnosis.

Sono Mammography

Specialized ultrasound imaging for breast health assessment and cancer screening.
